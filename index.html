Exam Day Strategy

Apply this framework in both January and April attempts

🔢
Numericals First

Attempt ALL 10 numerical questions per subject — zero negative marking. Risk-free every attempt. These alone can yield 40 marks.

✅
Selective MCQ

Only attempt HIGH-CONFIDENCE MCQs. Skip uncertain ones — negative marking (-1) makes guessing costly. Accuracy beats coverage.

⏱
2.5-Minute Cap

Never spend more than 2.5 minutes on any single question. Mark it, skip it, return only if time remains at the end.

📋
Subject Order

Start with your strongest subject for confidence. End with Chemistry — fastest to scan and easiest to review in the last 10 minutes.
